About me! I am a researcher and an academician. Currently, a Ph.D. student at Macquarie University. Tutoring undergrade/graduate students at Macquarie University, along with working as causal diploma teacher in Macquarie University International College. I have received MS degree in Computer Science from NUCES, Pakistan.

Research Interests. My research are Evolutionary Algorithms, Hyper-heuristics, Combinatorial Optimization, Search Heuristics, Multi and Many-Objective Algorithms, Reinforcement Learning .
